Our story

Small room, slow mornings, good coffee.

We started Hearth & Bean with one idea: a coffee shop should feel like the warmest room on the street. We roast in small batches, pull every shot to order, and bake first thing so the smell of it greets you at the door.

There's room for a laptop and a deadline, or a long catch-up with a friend. Either way, the second cup is on the house-warm side of things — stay as long as you like.

Watch us pour

Every cup, made to order.

No conveyor belts, no shortcuts — just fresh beans, steady hands, and a little patience.
